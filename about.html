ABOUT US

Ru Davril is a story of creativity passed through generations. The journey began in 1993 when my mother, Chandra Mehta, started designing from home, building her work with passion, craftsmanship, and dedication. Growing up in that environment, creativity became a natural part of my life.

I, Nishi Jhaveri, am a self-taught designer who continues this legacy with a contemporary perspective. At Ru Davril, we create clothing that is timeless yet modern — ranging from minimal, sophisticated silhouettes to richly embellished pieces, each designed to celebrate the spirit and individuality of the modern Indian woman.

Every collection begins with an inspiration that evolves into a concept. We blend Indian craftsmanship with contemporary global influences to create pieces that feel effortless, elegant, and versatile. Customisation remains an important part of our process, as we believe that what you wear is a true expression of who you are.

Recently, my daughter-in-law Saanya Jhaveri joined the team, bringing fresh energy and new ideas. Together, we look forward to continuing this journey and growing the brand while staying rooted in creativity and craftsmanship.

The greatest joy in my work comes from seeing women feel confident and comfortable in our creations. For me, the most exciting part of being a designer is the opportunity to create something new every single day.
